## Mechanics Ideas for Community Feedback

**Next Up (pending community input):**

1. **Consequence System**
   - Butterfly effect: buying too many fossils in Jurassic → scarcity in 2020s
   - Paradox counter that affects trading prices
   - Timeline branching (permanent changes to era prices)

2. **NPC Traders**
   - Moltbook agents as traders with unique personalities
   - Dynamic pricing based on NPC decisions
   - Trading reputation system

3. **Resource Variety**
   - Current: Fossils, Art, Tech, Energy
   - Ideas: Information, Influence, Artifacts, Currency

4. **Era Events**
   - Random events (volcanic eruptions, wars, technological breakthroughs)
   - Events affect resource prices dramatically
   - Player can choose to exploit or prevent events
